How to use the HTML Ada83 LRM

This document is the text of the Ada LRM, reformatted and indexed with hyper-text links. Different types of links are provided to accommodate different uses of the document.

Navigation buttons

Each section begins with one, two, or all three of the icons pictured below.
The PREV icon will take you to the previous subsection at this level (e.g. 3.3 to 3.2). The NEXT icon will take you to the next subsection (e.g. 3.2 to 3.3) The UP icon will take you to next highest level (e.g. 3.3 to 3). To move into a deeper level, look for entries in the current section or simply scroll down.

Index and Table of Contents buttons

Each section ends with these two icons:

The INDEX icon will take you directly to the Index, while the CONTENTS icon will take you to the Table of Contents.

In-line section references

All in-line references to other sections are live hyperlinks, so any time you see something like "(see 3.8.2)" you can click on the section number to go directly to the associated section.
